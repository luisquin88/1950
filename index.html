Montreal

I lived in Montreal for a year between 2012 to 2013. It's been so far my favorite place I've lived, there's so much art, vibrant culture, interesting people, amazing food, history, entertainment, etc. I lived in a part of Montreal called Le Plateau, right infront of a park called La Fontaine, it was really convenient of a location as I had everything I needed nearby and it was also a really beautiful area with old architecture, giant murals, lots of places to eat, and a lot of little interesting shops. I can honestly say I liked just about everything about living in Montreal, rent and food was cheaper than Vancouver and there's so much to see and do. Even the winter I spent there wasn't uncomfortable as it was a novelty for me to see so much snow and feel what -40 degrees feels like on your face. If anything the one thing that was uncomfortable was how humid it was in the summer, Montreal is an island so it gets pretty muggy when you're surrounded by water in hot climate.

Personal Rating:

Things to do8 out of 10
Hospitality7 out of 10
Food9 out of 10
Weather5 out of 10

Varadero

I took a trip to Varadero, Cuba with a few friends in 2013. Flying there from Montreal is actually pretty cheap compared to flying from the west coast. Cuba is one of my favorite places I've ever visited, and because its so small you get to see a lot in a small amount of time. We stayed at a resort called Playa Caleta, a 4-star all inclusive resort overlooking the beach. We took a few day trips while we were there, including snorkeling, horseback riding, cave exploring, off-roading, and a tour of Habana, a cigar factory, a market, and some historical buildings. The weather was perfect the whole time, warm but not scorching hot. The sea was also really call but we had heard that there had been an influx of jellyfish due to a prior cold front, luckily none of us got stung. I had no complaints about Cuba, everyone was friendly and I had fun the entire trip, I'm hoping to go back soon.

Personal Rating:

Things to do9 out of 10
Hospitality7 out of 10
Food10 out of 10
Weather10 out of 10
